Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download an app?

Simply click the "Download on App Store" button on any product page. You'll be redirected to the App Store where you can download the app for free.

Are the apps free?

Most of our apps are free to download and use. Some apps may offer premium features or ad-free versions through in-app purchases.

What devices are supported?

All our apps are designed for iOS devices including iPhone and iPad. Check each app's page on the App Store for specific device requirements.
