How it all began

Our story.

From one app built to solve a real problem, to a growing portfolio of native iOS apps people use every day.

It all started with one simple problem: tracking shared expenses with friends during camping trips and events.

We began with a single app: Group Expenses Light. The idea was born during my graduate school years, when I organized camping trips, picnics, and shared birthday gifts with friends.

The challenge was always the same: at the end of each event, someone had to figure out the complicated math of who owes whom and how much. It was time-consuming and often led to confusion.

We thought: why not let our phones do the hard work while we enjoy the event? That's where the brainstorming began.

From One App to Many

After launching Group Expenses Light, we listened to our users and created Group Expenses Plus with enhanced features like unequal splits, custom currencies, and advanced visibility controls.

The success of these apps inspired us to keep creating. Today, we offer a diverse portfolio of apps spanning entertainment, puzzles, productivity tools, and educational games.
