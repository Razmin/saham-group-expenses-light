Our Commitment to Privacy

At NovaNext, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect. This policy explains what anonymous data we collect across our mobile applications and how we use it to improve your experience.

Privacy First: We collect only anonymous data that cannot be linked to you personally. We never collect names, emails, or personal information.

Data We Collect

Anonymous Usage Data

  • App Usage Statistics: How often you use our apps, which features you access
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and screen resolution
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase transactions (product IDs, purchase dates, transaction status)
  • Game Statistics: High scores, levels completed, gameplay patterns, and puzzle solving times
  • Math Calculations: Types of calculations performed (for math apps) without storing actual data
  • File Processing: File types (for CSV/data apps) without storing content
  • Camera Usage: Photo/video capture frequency (for camera apps) without storing media

What We Do NOT Collect

Important: We never collect personal information that could identify you.
  • Personal Information: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details
  • Account Information: User credentials, passwords, or personal account data
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, precise location, or location history
  • Content Data: Photos, videos, documents, CSV files, or any user-generated content (all processing is done locally)
  • Personal Files: Gallery photos, camera images, or documents you process in our apps
  • Calendar Data: Personal dates, events, or calendar information
  • Mathematical Data: Actual numbers, equations, or calculations you perform
  • Browsing History: Web browsing data or search queries
  • Biometric Data: Fingerprints, facial recognition, or other biometric identifiers

How We Use Anonymous Data

App Improvement

  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • Optimize app performance and loading times
  • Understand which features are most popular
  • Guide development of new features and game levels

Business Analytics

  • Track overall app usage trends
  • Analyze purchase patterns to improve our products
  • Understand regional market performance
  • Measure the success of app updates

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

  • All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates to protect data integrity
  • Industry-standard security protocols and practices

Data Retention

  • Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 2 years
  • Aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics and app improvement:

Analytics Services

  • Purpose: To analyze app usage and performance
  • Data Shared: Anonymous usage statistics and technical data
  • Privacy: All third-party services comply with strict privacy standards

App Store Analytics

  • Purpose: To understand app store performance and user acquisition
  • Data Shared: Download statistics and regional performance data
  • Privacy: All data is provided by Apple and Google in anonymized form

Your Rights and Controls

Data Control

  • All data collection is anonymous and cannot be linked to you personally
  • You can disable analytics in your device settings

Opt-Out Options

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements > Don't Share

Children's Privacy

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Our apps are designed to be family-friendly
  • If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately
  • We will promptly delete any data identified as belonging to a child
