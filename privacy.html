Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information NovaNext apps may collect and how it is used.

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Overview

NovaNext develops mobile applications for iPhone and iPad. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing high-quality apps and services.

Information We Collect

Some NovaNext apps may collect anonymous usage and diagnostic information to help improve app performance and user experience.

  • App Usage Statistics: How often our apps are used and which features are accessed.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, language settings, and screen resolution.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase transaction details such as product identifiers, purchase dates, and transaction status.
  • Game Statistics: High scores, completed levels, gameplay patterns, and puzzle-solving times.
  • Math Calculation Statistics: Types of calculations performed in math-related apps without storing actual values.
  • File Processing Information: File types processed by data-related apps without storing file contents.
  • Camera Usage Statistics: Frequency of photo or video capture actions without storing captured media.
  • Location Information: GPS coordinates and location-related information when required by app functionality and permitted by the user.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Passwords or account credentials
  • Payment card or banking information
  • Photos, videos, or documents stored on your device
  • User-generated content created within our apps
Many NovaNext apps perform calculations, puzzle solving, photo processing, and other tasks directly on your device. The content you create is not collected by NovaNext.

How We Use Anonymous Data

App Improvement

  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes.
  • Optimize app performance and loading times.
  • Understand which features are most popular.
  • Guide development of new features and game levels.

Business Analytics

  • Track overall app usage trends.
  • Analyze purchase patterns to improve our products.
  • Understand regional market performance.
  • Measure the success of app updates.

Security Measures

Data Protection

  • All data is encrypted during transmission and storage.
  • Secure industry-standard technologies are used where applicable.
  • We work with trusted service providers that maintain security and privacy standards.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics and app improvement.

Analytics Services

  • Purpose: Analyze app usage and performance.
  • Data Shared: Anonymous usage statistics and technical data.
  • Privacy: Third-party providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

Examples include Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

App Store Analytics

  • Purpose: Understand app store performance and user acquisition.
  • Data Shared: Download statistics and regional performance data.
  • Privacy: Data provided by Apple is aggregated or anonymized.

Your Rights and Controls

Data Control

  • All data collection is anonymous and cannot be directly linked to you personally.
  • You can disable analytics in your device settings.

Analytics Preferences

iOS:
Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised Last Updated date.
